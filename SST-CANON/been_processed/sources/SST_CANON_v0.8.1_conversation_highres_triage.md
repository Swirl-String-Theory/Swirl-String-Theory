   - `r_c` remains defined by `r_c = v_swirl / omega_c`.
   - `omega_c` is the electron Compton angular frequency, `m_e c^2 / hbar`.
   - `r_c` should be read as `R_horn`, a horn/circulation/return-flow radius.
   - The resolved vortex-tube radius is `a_core`, not `r_c`.

2. **Density closure correction**
   - The old `rho_core ~ m_e c^2/(2 pi v_swirl^2 r_c^3)` must be reworded as an effective horn-envelope density normalization.
   - Do not identify it with a local material density unless a resolved tube profile is introduced.

   - Attachment Lemma remains an open gate: exact global holonomy `chi` is required.

4. **Trefoil sector discipline**
   - Trefoil remains electron/positron lepton calibration.
   - Do not use trefoil for quark fractional charge.
