Triple gear split click-fit kit

Source mesh:
  triple_gear_solid_with_mark.stl

What this kit does:
  - Splits each of the 3 linked rings into 2 printable halves.
  - Adds connector pairs at each cut face.
  - Connector style: press/snap-fit pegs on half A, mating sockets on half B.

Important:
  - This is a best-effort engineering split based directly on the uploaded mesh.
  - Gear 3 is generated by 120-degree symmetry rotation of gear 1.
  - Snap behavior depends on printer tolerance and material.
  - For a looser fit, lightly sand the pegs or enlarge sockets by ~0.1 to 0.2 mm.
