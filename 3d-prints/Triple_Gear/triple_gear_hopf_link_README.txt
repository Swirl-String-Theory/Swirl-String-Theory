Triple gear Hopf-link smooth printable model
Based on Schleimer & Segerman, Triple gear (2013).
This is the linked torus base geometry only; exact gear teeth are not reconstructed.
Core radius: 30.000 mm
Tube radius: 9.387 mm
Nominal clearance: 0.600 mm
Files: .py for Blender, .obj and .stl for direct import/printing.
