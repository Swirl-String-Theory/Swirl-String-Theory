Parametric fitted-lane triple gear v1

This is a clean parametric reconstruction driven by:
- the published Hopf-link placement from Schleimer & Segerman
- a similarity fit to the uploaded STL
- fitted tooth-lane phases and counts extracted from the uploaded mesh

What is exact here:
- 3-fold symmetric Hopf-link placement
- major radius / center radius scale fit
- 3 tooth lanes per ring
- 16-tooth periodicity per lane
- lane beta positions anchored to the paper

What is still approximate:
- outer-carving envelope is represented by a smooth parametric tooth bump model
  rather than a full exact swept-envelope reconstruction.
