Why This Matters

Energetic Stability

The "standard" view. Systems evolve until forces balance. Like a pendulum stopping at the bottom. This requires energy dissipation.

∞

Topological Stability

The "fluid" view. The system is stuck in a high-energy state because the path to the bottom is blocked by conservation laws (Helicity). It's stable not because it's at the bottom, but because it's trapped.
