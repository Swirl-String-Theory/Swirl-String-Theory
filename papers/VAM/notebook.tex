    \[
        \mathcal{L}_{\text{int}} = -g\, \bar{\Psi}_i^\alpha (\gamma^\mu)_{\alpha\beta} (G_\mu)^{ij} \Psi_j^\beta

    \[
        \text{\small where } i,j \in \{\text{r},\text{g},\text{b}\}, \quad \alpha,\beta \in \{\rr ,2,3,4\}
    \]

    \bigskip

    \noindent
    This can be written as a \textbf{color block matrix}:
    \[
        \bar{\Psi} \gamma^\mu G_\mu \Psi =
        \begin{bmatrix}
            \bar{\psi}_{r\alpha} & \bar{\psi}_{g\alpha} & \bar{\psi}_{b\alpha}
        \end{bmatrix}
        \cdot
        \begin{bmatrix}
            \gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta} G_\mu^{\rr \rr } & \cdots & \gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta} G_\mu^{\rr 3} \\
            \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
            \gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta} G_\mu^{3\rr } & \cdots & \gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta} G_\mu^{33}
        \end{bmatrix}
        \cdot
        \begin{bmatrix}
            \psi_{r\beta} \\ \psi_{g\beta} \\ \psi_{b\beta}
        \end{bmatrix}
    \]
