Relational Time-of-Arrival (TOA) Simulator

Interactive visualization of covariant field observables. Observe how the intrinsic width of a quantum wavepacket (σ_t) and the variance of a physical clock (σ_τ) combine to broaden the measured arrival time distribution.

🎛️ Parameters

1.0

Intrinsic spread of the incoming particle.

0.5

Quantum fluctuations of the detector's physical clock.

📐 Theory

The total observed variance is the sum of the wavepacket variance and the clock variance (Eq 8.16):

Var(Θ) = σ_t² + σ_τ²
